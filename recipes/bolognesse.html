Simple Bolognesse Sauce

A simple take on the traditional guacamole recipe. A super fresh, zesty mexican dip that is sure to impress!

A staple in mexican cuisine, Guacamole has stood the test of time as a dip adored by various walks of life throughout its lifetime.

Ingredients

  • Two whole avacados
  • One shallot
  • Three garlic cloves
  • One roasted jalapeño
  • One whole lime
  • Salt (to taste)
  • One teaspoon of cumin power

Method

  1. Finely chop one shallot, three garlic cloves and once roasted jalapeño
  2. Add your vegetables to a bowl along with 1tsp of cumin powder and two avacados diced
  3. Mash together with a fork until combined and taste for salt
  4. Add the juice of half a lime and mix well
