Easy Homemade Steak

A recipe for a steakhouse quality steak, but in your own home!

Professional chefs over exaggerate how difficult cooking a beautiful steak is, however in reality there are multiple easy things you can do to improve your steaks at home

Before delving into the process, first we need to discuss the star of the show: the steak

You dont need to go and spend $100 on a good cut of beef, as with the right technique and care, even a choice cut can turn out amazing

The key points when preparing the steak are:

  • Allowing it to come up to room temp
  • Applying salt & pepper ~5min before cooking
  • Ensuring you're maintaining a consistent heat on the steak throughout cooking

Ingredients

  • Quality cut of beef
  • Olive oil
  • Two garlic cloves
  • Sprigs of rosemery & thyme
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Butter

Method

  1. Bring your pan up to medium high heat
  2. Add 1 tsp of olive oil
  3. Place steak firmly, layin away from you
  4. Start timer depending on how cooked you want the steak
    • Rare: 2min each side
    • Medium Rare: 3min each side
    • Medium: 4min each side
    • Medium Well: 5min each side
    • Well Done: 6min each side
  5. Once the first side is done flip
  6. After halfway through the cook time for the second side, add your butter, garlic and herbs
  7. Baste the steak in the butter consatntly throughout the duration of the remaining time
  8. Take the steak off the pan and onto a plate, pouring the contents of the pan over it
  9. Let the steak rest for half of the total cook time
